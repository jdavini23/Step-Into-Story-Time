⚙️ Pricing Tier Breakdown

🟦 1. Free Tier – “Starter Magic”

Ideal for: New users, casual storytellers
Goal: Hook users with basic functionality and emotional connection

Features:
	•	3 stories per week
	•	Basic personalization (child’s name, 1 trait)
	•	3 popular themes (bedtime, fantasy, adventure)
	•	Save last 3 stories
	•	Read online (text-only)
	•	Prompt to upgrade after each story

Conversion lever: Free users get value but run into friction quickly (story limit, lack of illustration/voice, limited personalization).

⸻

🟨 2. Premium Tier – “Storytime Plus” (~$6–9/mo or $59/year)

Ideal for: Parents who read stories regularly and want deeper engagement
Goal: Monetize core power users through rich personalization and visuals

Features:
	•	Unlimited stories
	•	Full personalization (name, traits, family members, interests)
	•	Access to all story genres
	•	Multiple story lengths (short, medium, long)
	•	AI-generated illustrations (1 per story)
	•	Save and organize full story library
	•	Printable PDFs
	•	Scheduled story delivery (email or app push)
	•	Premium support

Conversion lever: Unlocks the real value of the platform — highly personalized, emotionally engaging content parents can’t replicate elsewhere.

⸻

🟩 3. Family Tier – “Storytime Pro” (~$12–15/mo or $119/year)

Ideal for: Families with multiple kids or those who want full immersion
Goal: Capture high-value households and offer “wow” features

**Includes everything in Plus, and:
	•	Add multiple child profiles
	•	Voice narration with character voices
	•	Interactive endings (child chooses path)
	•	Milestone stories (birthdays, holidays, life events)
	•	Magic Letters (from characters or parents)
	•	Bonus: “Design your character” for AI to use in stories (optional avatar/visual builder)
	•	Priority feature voting or early access to new modes

Conversion lever: Premium storytelling as an emotional memory tool, not just entertainment. Great for gifting too.

⸻

🧠 Add-on Ideas (Optional Upsells)

You can test these as one-time purchases or in-app purchases:
	•	🎁 Personalized printed storybook
	•	🖼️ Illustration bundle pack (e.g., download 5 custom images)
	•	📬 Story subscription gift pass (send to a friend for 1–3 months)

⸻

💡 Positioning Tips
	•	Free tier = “Your child’s first magical story”
	•	Plus = “Your child’s favorite characters, personalized”
	•	Pro = “An immersive story world your family will treasure”
